Our team

We are a group of five IFE students teaming up to promote sustainability goals and find best solution to chosen problem using PBL techniques.

Our team is called E-JOPIA. E standing for using computer technologies in our project, for example remote meetings on Teams platform or using virtual tools to create diagrams.

Each letter of JOPIA is the first letter of our names:

  • J - Jan
  • O - Oliwia
  • P - Patryk
  • I - Islam
  • A - Artur
Our team logo presents vision of changing people mindset, to use public transport instead of cars.
